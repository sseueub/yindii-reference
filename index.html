What We Do

Yindii helps stores sell surplus food to nearby consumers,
reducing waste while creating additional revenue.

  • Reduce food waste
  • Create incremental sales
  • Drive new customer discovery
  • Protect brand image
Pilot Structure

How the alpha test works — store to consumer, end to end.

Partner Onboarding
Selected F&B stores join the pilot. Surplus inventory is registered on the Yindii platform each day.
